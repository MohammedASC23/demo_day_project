Red flags to look out for:





: Being told to download random links


: Calls/Messages sent by an unkwown number


: Websites that have http in the URL and not https


: You are asked for personal information without a good reason


: If things happen out of the blue and are too good to be true


Note: If you are ever suspicious or sketched out by anything that isn't an immeadiate red flag, ask others for advice and be careful
