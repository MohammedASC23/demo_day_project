Website Origin

The reason we created this website is because we had seen people in our lives be subjects to scamming and how it affected them financially and emotionally. In particular, Zubayer's grandpa, Abdul had been a victim to scamming online and ended up losing a lot of money and was put into a difficult situation. Due to this, we had seen the impact that scamming can cause and created a website to help people just like Abdul. We hope that this website will help those who have been victims to scamming understand how to deal with the problem.
